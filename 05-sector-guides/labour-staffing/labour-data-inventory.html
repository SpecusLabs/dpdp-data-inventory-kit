⚡ Priority Actions for Staffing Businesses

  • Replace WhatsApp document collection with a digital intake form (consent required — Section 6)
  • Audit Google Drive and Google Sheet sharing settings — remove unnecessary access today
  • Stop using personal recruiter email for candidate data — move to company email
  • Issue DPA with background verification agency before next BGV run
  • Delete Aadhaar and PAN scans after statutory registration is complete
  • Set 12-month deletion trigger for non-placed candidates
