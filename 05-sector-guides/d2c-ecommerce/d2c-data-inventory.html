⚡ Priority Actions for D2C Businesses

  • Add consent checkbox at Shopify checkout — most D2C stores are missing this (DPDP Act Section 6)
  • Issue DPAs with Shiprocket and Klaviyo — two most commonly unaddressed processors
  • Move HR/payroll data out of unencrypted spreadsheets
  • Implement cookie consent banner for GA4 and Meta Pixel
  • Document and test your customer erasure request process (Section 12)
