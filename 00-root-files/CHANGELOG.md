## [1.0.0] — 2025-05-23

### Added
- Initial release of the DPDP Data Inventory Kit
- `01-dpdp-reference`: Key DPDP Act sections for data inventory; personal data categories under the Act
- `02-data-inventory-framework`: Master inventory (CSV + HTML), lite inventory (CSV), and framework guide
- `03-data-mapping-templates`: System, vendor, and data-subject mapping templates (CSV + HTML)
- `04-data-flow-diagrams`: Interactive HTML flow diagram template and text-based checklist
- `05-sector-guides`: Sector-specific inventories and flow guides for D2C e-commerce, labour staffing, and AI SaaS
- Full DPDP Act section references throughout all templates
